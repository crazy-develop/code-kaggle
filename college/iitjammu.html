🎓 Establishment

Founded in 2016 and initially mentored by IIT Delhi.

Campus Size: Sprawling 400-acre modern campus at Jagti.

🏆 National Ranking

Ranked 62nd in Engineering (NIRF 2024).

📘 Programs Offered

  • B.Tech: Multiple specializations (JEE Advanced required).
  • M.Tech: Specialized engineering disciplines.
  • Ph.D.: Doctoral research across several fields.

📝 Admission Eligibility

B.Tech: At least 75% in 10+2 and a competitive JEE Advanced score.

🏢 Infrastructure & Research

  • Modern classrooms and 375-seater auditorium.
  • SAPTARSHI Labs: Central Instrumentation Facility.
  • Dedicated Innovation Centers.

🏓 Student Life

  • Full hostels, dining hall, and gym.
  • Football, basketball, badminton facilities.
  • Active student clubs and organizations.

💼 Placement Success

Placement Rate: 85%+ overall success rate.

Students placed in top national and international firms.

💰 Fees & Scholarships

Tuition fees vary based on parental income.

Fee waivers available for SC/ST and families with income < ₹5,00,000.
