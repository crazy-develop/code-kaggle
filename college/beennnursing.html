🎓 Key Info

Established: 1998

Affiliation: University of Jammu

Type: Private Nursing College

✅ Approvals

Approved by Indian Nursing Council (INC), J&K State Nursing Council, and J&K State Government.

📘 Degree Courses

  • B.Sc. Nursing: 4-year degree course.
  • Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing: 2-year degree for registered nurses.
  • M.Sc. Nursing: Postgraduate degree.

📜 Diploma Course

GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery): Offered through J&K Paramedical & Nursing Council.

💰 Fees Structure

  • B.Sc. Nursing: ₹3,00,000 (Entire 4-year course)
  • Post Basic B.Sc.: ₹1,50,000 (2-year course)
  • GNM Diploma: ₹6,00,000 (Total course fee)

📝 Educational Criteria

Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB) and English.

Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved category).

⚖️ Requirements

  • Age: 17 to 35 Years.
  • Domicile: J&K UT or Ladakh.
  • Medical fitness mandatory.
