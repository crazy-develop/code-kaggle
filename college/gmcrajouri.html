🏢 Healthcare Infrastructure

  • Associated Hospital: 300+ beds wala functional hospital clinical training ke liye.
  • Central Library: Medical journals, textbooks aur e-library facility.
  • Hostels: Boys aur Girls ke liye separate secure hostels.
  • Labs: Anatomy, Physiology aur Biochemistry ke high-tech labs.
  • Smart Halls: Smart classrooms with Projectors/Speakers.
  • Co-curricular: Auditorium aur Gym facility.

🛠️ Admission Trigger

  • NEET-UG: MBBS admissions ke liye NEET-UG qualify karna mandatory hai.
  • Seat Pool: 15% All India Quota (AIQ) & 85% State Quota (JKBOPEE).
