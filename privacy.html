Privacy Policy

Suvidha AI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Suvidha.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account or profile
  • Book a session with a mentor
  • Use our institution comparison tools
  • Sign up for our newsletter

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our platform
  • Connect you with appropriate mentors
  • Send you updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Personalize your experience through AI-driven insights

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Verified mentors you connect with
  • Service providers who assist our operations
  • Authorities if required by law

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can update your account information, manage your notification preferences, or request to delete your data by contacting us.
